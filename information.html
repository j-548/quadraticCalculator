Understanding the Quadratic Formula

  • Calculator
  • More Information

The standard form of a Quadratiic Equation looks like this: ax2 + bx + c = 0. Values a, b, and c are known and a cannot be 0. The purpose is to solve for the variable "x". There are several ways to find the solutions. The calculator on the "calculator" page handles all of this for us. Insert a number in inputs a, b, and c. Then click calculate to get the solutions.
